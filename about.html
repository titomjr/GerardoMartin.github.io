About Gerardo Martin

I am a diverse programmer with experience in web design, machine learning, and simple coding projects. My love for solving complex problems led me to pursue a Bachelors Degree in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Computer Engineering.
Graduated from Texas State University in December 2023, I built a foundation of coding principles and project management throughout my time. I have honed my skills in a variety of programming languages such as C/C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I am also experienced with MATLAB, CAD design, and SPICE simulators.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life by developing simple solutions for complex problems with efficiency in mind.

In my spare time I enjoy writing music, painting, and woodworking.
